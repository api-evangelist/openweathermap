In July 2022, the UK experienced record heat that melted road surfaces and exposed infrastructure vulnerabilities, as Northern European roads designed for temperate climates fail when bitumen softens and asphalt bleeds under extreme temperatures. OpenWeather's Road Weather Intelligence enables proactive maintenance through surface-temperature forecasting, letting authorities dispatch crews and implement restrictions before pavement fails.
